Bipolar Disorder Quiz

1. Do you have periods where you feel excessively happy or irritable, lasting for at least one week?

2. Do you feel unusually confident or have grandiose ideas during these periods?

3. Do you have decreased need for sleep (e.g., feel rested after only a few hours of sleep)?

4. Do you talk more than usual or feel a pressure to keep talking?

5. Do you have racing thoughts or experience a flight of ideas?

6. Are you easily distracted?

7. Do you engage in risky behaviors, such as spending sprees, sexual indiscretions, or foolish investments, during these periods?

8. Do you also experience periods of feeling very sad or empty, and lose interest in most activities?

9. Do you have significant weight changes or changes in appetite?

10. Do you have trouble sleeping or sleep too much?
