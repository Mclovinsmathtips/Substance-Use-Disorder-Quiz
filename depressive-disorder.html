Depressive Disorder Quiz

1. Have you experienced a depressed mood most of the day, nearly every day?

2. Have you experienced a marked loss of interest or pleasure in all, or almost all, activities most of the day, nearly every day?

3. Have you experienced significant weight loss when not dieting, weight gain, or decrease or increase in appetite nearly every day?

4. Have you experienced insomnia or hypersomnia nearly every day?
